Hospital Transaction

In my recent Power BI project, I conducted an in-depth analysis of a hospital's transaction data spanning three years (2021-2023), focusing on key metrics such as revenue, expenses, profitability, doctor performance, and patient demographics. Through meticulous data familiarization, transformation, dimensional analysis, and KPI calculations using DAX formulas, I uncovered significant insights. The hospital demonstrated robust financial health with a $274,000 revenue and 31% profit margin. Patient trends revealed intriguing fluctuations, warranting further investigation into factors like service quality and competitor activity. Additionally, departmental performance analysis highlighted Dermatology, Radiology, and Paediatrics as the most profitable units. Recommendations include investigating the 2023 decline in patient turnout, optimizing departmental performance, boosting patient retention through targeted campaigns, and leveraging predictive analytics for future resource allocation. This project showcases the power of data-driven insights in improving hospital operations and patient care.
